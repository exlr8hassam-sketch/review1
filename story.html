✦ How It Started

A Passion Born from Beads

Hi! I'm Misbah Iqbal, and I'm the heart & hands behind Twinkles by Anjum. What started as a quiet hobby of stringing beads during free afternoons in Multan quickly grew into something I never expected — a brand loved by thousands.

Every bracelet I make, every phone charm I craft, every gift set I assemble — each one carries a piece of my heart. I believe jewellery isn't just an accessory; it's a feeling. A tiny piece of art you carry with you every day. 💕

✦ The Process

Made by Hand, Sent with Love

Every single piece in the Twinkles by Anjum collection is handmade from scratch — no factories, no machines, just me and my beads. I source the prettiest materials I can find: soft pearls, shimmering crystals, vibrant seed beads, and more.

I pour hours of love into each creation, making sure every knot is tight, every bead is perfectly placed, and every piece looks exactly as beautiful in person as it does in photos. Because you deserve nothing less. ✨
